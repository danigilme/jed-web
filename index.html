Is Jed a website service or an app?+
Jed is an iPhone app. Your website, domain, email and all the daily SEO work live behind one app on your phone: change prices, add photos, approve pages and watch your rankings from anywhere. No laptop needed, ever. Android comes later, once iPhone is flying.
Do I have to write anything or manage it myself?+
No. Jed reads your business from your Google Maps listing and works on autopilot: it builds the site, writes the pages, posts to your Google Business Profile and replies to reviews. You can approve things from the app if you want to, or just let it fly.
How fast will I see results?+
Your website and domain are live on day one, and you’ll see Jed’s work in your feed every single day. Rankings compound: most local businesses start seeing movement in 4 to 8 weeks, with the big gains building over months. That’s why Jed ships one growth action daily: local SEO is won by showing up every day, not by a one-time setup.
What if I already have a website?+
Jed rebuilds it better: faster, mobile-first and structured the way Google and AI assistants expect. If you already own a domain, you keep it and Jed takes over the heavy lifting from there.
Do I really own the domain and the site?+
Yes. The domain is registered in your name, not ours. If you ever leave, you take the domain, the website and every page Jed wrote with you. No hostage-taking. That is the whole point of owning your channel.
How is this different from Thumbtack or Angi?+
Lead brokers charge you per lead, sell the same customer to several pros, and own the relationship. Jed is the opposite: one flat monthly price to build an asset you own: your site, your rankings, your calls, direct from customers. Every month of Jed makes your business stronger, not a marketplace’s.
What does it cost?+
One plan: $99/month, everything included. No contracts, no setup fees, no per-lead fees, cancel anytime. Compare that to one Thumbtack lead ($30 to $80, shared with five other pros) or an SEO agency retainer ($1,500+/month) and the math does itself: one extra job a month covers Jed.

Jed is iPhone-first and coming soon to the App Store. Email hello@jed.so to get on the launch list: you’ll be the first in, and founding pricing gets locked in from day one.
